Niels, also known by his artist name "Khasilen", was born on a specific day within 1994, in one of the most beautiful natural parks of the Netherlands. He now lives in Utrecht; a historic yet modern and progressive city, where he studies music composition at the Utrecht Conservatory.
Niels was introduced into the world of (melodic) percussion by Wibrand van Norel, who taught him the trades of playing the marimba and vibraphone, while also teaching him the basics of writing music.
Apart from writing music Niels thoroughly enjoys long and pointless walks, graphical design, cinematography and envisioning the world as a giant canvas. "Keep your eyes and ears open; the world both exsists in, and revolves around art."
